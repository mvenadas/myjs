Basic Operators

Operators allows you to transform or combine multiple values. It can be used for simple math or more complex equations.

Math Operators
const ageMichael = 2023 - 1979;
Answer: 44
The "-" subtracts the two years.

Example of adding more operators to a console log:
console.log((ageMichael * ageRosie) / 10, 2 ** 3);
Note: 2 ** 3 means 2 to the power of 3 = 2 * 2 *2

console.log(firstName + ' ' + lastName);
Answer: Michael Venadas

Assignment Operators
let x = 10 + 5;
console.log(x);
Answer: 15

x += 10;
Note: x = x + 10
Answer: 25

x *= 4
Note: x = x * 4
Answer: 100

x++;
Note: x = x + 1
Answer: 101

x--;
Note: x = x - 1
Answer: 100

Comparison Operators
console.log(ageMichael > ageRosie);
Answer: true

Note: Other operators
> Greater than
< Less than
>= Greater than or equal to
<= Less than or equal to
